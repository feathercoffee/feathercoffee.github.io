About

After being diagnosed with a rare lung disease, Melanee decided she wanted to provide a consistent way to raise funds for those affected by rare diseases. Subsequently, she founded Feather Coffee, a mobile-coffee company that gives a percentage of its profits to rare disease organizations. To help raise start-up funds, she is embarking on a Kickstarter campaign that will go live April 29.

Her vision for the future of Feather Coffee is to develop the business model she calls "cause-brewing" into a franchise so that more people can make a difference in the fight against rare diseases. People in the rare disease community can buy a Feather Coffee trailer and raise money for their cause in a consistent and flexible way.

Team

Melanee Grondahl

Founder Feather Coffee

Melanee was diagnosed with a rare lung disease called Lymphangioleiomyomatosis or LAM in April 2013. She transformed her diagnosis into a warrant for a new life and decided to use her creativity, business acumen, and passion for the rare disease community to start Feather Coffee. A former English instructor and digital content designer, she is now a rare disease advocate who is determined to give hope to the rare disease community one sip at a time. Read more of Melanee's Story.
